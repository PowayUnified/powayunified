Review Edlio features, describing what you can edit with additional links to the Edlio Help Center.

## Images

Images can be edited in the image shuffle. Read more about **Shuffles**
<a href="https://help.edlio.com/apps/pages/shuffles" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">here</a>.

- ALWAYS enter **ALT Text** for images to meet ADA compliance
- School sites: hero photo shuffle
- Background image 
- School safety and belonging photos
- District: our schools and programs
- District: social media photos
- The photos in the dropdown menu are accessible via the site navigation

## Spotlight Messages

You are able to edit the text, images, and the links to where the Spotlight Messages link to on your end in the admin. Read more about **Spotlight Messages**
<a href="https://help.edlio.com/apps/pages/edit-spotlight-messages" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">here</a>.

- School motto/tagline under shuffle
- 3 photos with text overlapping image  (about us, school profile, class of 2026/K-5)
- District & secondary schools: About Poway message + photo
- District: learn more about Poway Unified message

## Quick Links

The quick links are edited using the Links feature. Read more about **Links**
<a href="https://help.edlio.com/apps/pages/links" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">here</a>.

- Find it Fast Links
- District: quick link overlapping video
- Schools: quick links with icons overlapping shuffle
- District: our schools and programs links
- Schools: text box links
- School Safety and Belonging links
- Footer links
- Social media links
